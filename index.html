Our Story

TheHazyCollective is a group of creatives that were based in the UAE. Setting out in
Rotterdam, we are an established creative film production agency that focuses on private and
corporate shooting in various industries in The Netherlands.

We plan to expand TheHazyCollective in The Netherlands and as we grow, we will aim for
quality and creativity to ensure value and reach out for opportunities in the Arts, Music, and
Fashion Industry.

What we do?

TheHazyCollective is a Creative Film Production Agency based in Rotterdam, The Netherlands.

We do commercial and private shootings for individuals and corporations. Each project will be handled by
one of our team members.
